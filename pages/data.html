Why have we landed here?

Arguably, an understanding of the current state of the pandemic begins and ends with the data. As can be seen below, Colorado reached a peak of hospitalized covid-19 patients in mid April. Over 2 months of a gradual decline in hospitalized covid-19 numbers followed. However, since late June, the state has experienced a growth in the number of hospitalized patients. This should cause us all to redouble our efforts to wear a mask in public at all times (absolutely when indoors or when distancing from others cannot be maintained outdoors), maintain social distancing, wash hands frequently, and remain home when feeling unwell.

The bottom line is this, any one of us could spread the covid-19 virus to anyone else at any time, even in the absence of symptoms. We must all behave accordingly.
